Member-Get-Member Project

Referral Program: The Requirement

Existing customers can refer their friends via email address. The referred friend will receive an email and will need to create an account to the store to receive his/her reward.

Once the referred friend makes his/her first purchase, the referrer will receive an email on how to get their reward, too!

Referral Program: The Problem

The form will be hosted on a landing page within the store website. The feature is to be coded on Liquid and will not use an app.

  • How do we determine if the referrer is a customer on the store?
  • How do we save the referrer's email address?
  • How do we trigger the automated emails?

Referral Program: The Solution

On submit of the referral form, an account will be made for the referred email address.
The account will be tagged with "referral" and the email address of the referrer.

A separate server will handle the account checking for the referrer. The server will:

  • Check the referrer's email address if it's already on the store
  • Tag the referrer's account with "referrer"

For automated emails, Klaviyo app was used.
We created 2 Klaviyo Segment for accounts that are tagged "referral" and "referrer".
Automated emails were triggered by Klaviyo Flows whenever a profile is added to the segment.

Referral Program: The Product

A landing page for the Member-Get-Member feature was created wherein existing customers can enter their email address and their friend's details.

Email templates on Klaviyo app were created.
One for referrals which includes the a register button that goes straight to the website's account register page.
Another one for referrers that has their reward details.
