Custom Page template

Bärater page template

A completely custom page template that consists of questions and choices the customer can answer.

At the end of the question set, recommended products will show on the page based on the answers.

Landing page

The landing page consists of an image banner and some additional details.

The last section is the questionnaire.

Every question is displayed separately.
When an answer is selected, the Next button will enable and will let you proceed to the next section.

The answers accepted depends on the question.
Other questions accept multiple answers. It's also possible to de-select answers.

At the last question, the button switches to Submit.
Once clicked, it hides the question and displays a completely new section in its place.
The section shows the recommended products.

The products to be shown are based on a hard-coded logic of answer sets. It's possible to add the product directly to cart once it's shown.
